# App Opportunities Research

*Created: 2026-02-01*
*Last Updated: 2026-02-01*

## Overview

Research into high-performing single-purpose mobile apps, their revenue models, and customer pain points to identify opportunities.

---

## üì± 10 Apps Analyzed

### 1. Calm - Sleep & Meditation
- **Revenue:** ~$7.7M/month in-app, $596M valuation
- **Price:** $69.99/year or $14.99/month
- **Problem Solved:** Helps people sleep and relax
- **Trustpilot:** 1.5/5 ‚≠ê

**Top Pain Points:**
- Charged after cancellation (VERY HIGH)
- No phone support - email only, 30+ day response
- Free trial charges immediately
- Can't remove payment details from website
- Content hard to find/navigate
- Different prices on web vs app

**Opportunity:** Billing transparency, better cancellation flow, human support

---

### 2. Flighty - Flight Tracking
- **Revenue:** ~$400k/month, 200k+ downloads
- **Price:** $59.99/year or $149.99 lifetime
- **Problem Solved:** Know about delays before the airline

**Top Pain Points:**
- iOS only - no Android version (MAJOR GAP)
- Incoming flight tracking unreliable
- TripIt sync restrictions
- Price increased recently
- Can't import old flights easily

**Opportunity:** Android version = massive untapped market

---

### 3. CARROT Weather - Sarcastic Weather App
- **Revenue:** Est. $50k+/month (50,000+ paying users)
- **Price:** $4.99 upfront + $0.99-$9.99/year premium
- **Problem Solved:** Weather with personality

**Top Pain Points:**
- Android version inferior to iOS
- Radar doesn't work when zoomed in
- $5 app + $30/year feels expensive
- Layout has wasted space
- Apple's native weather catching up

**Opportunity:** Better Android parity, simpler pricing

---

### 4. Fabulous - Daily Routine Builder
- **Revenue:** Est. $2-5M+/year (Duke University backed)
- **Price:** $64.99/year or lifetime
- **Problem Solved:** Build healthy routines
- **Trustpilot:** Poor ratings

**Top Pain Points:**
- Charged despite cancellation (VERY HIGH)
- Misleading pricing/trial tactics
- Targets ADHD/anxiety users then frustrates them
- Intrusive pop-ups
- Glitchy - can't access daily goals

**Opportunity:** Honest billing, ADHD-friendly without manipulation

---

### 5. Streaks - Habit Tracker
- **Revenue:** Est. $1-2M+/year (Apple Design Award Winner)
- **Price:** $4.99 one-time purchase
- **Problem Solved:** Track up to 24 habits

**Top Pain Points:**
- UX is complicated
- Max 24 habits limit
- Streaks cause anxiety when broken
- No option to disable streak counting
- Apple only - no Android

**Opportunity:** Simpler UX, Android version, streak-free mode

---

### 6. Yuka - Food & Cosmetics Scanner ‚≠ê PRIORITY
- **Revenue:** Est. $5-10M/year (56M+ users)
- **Price:** Free / $14.99/year Premium
- **Problem Solved:** Scan products to see if healthy

**Top Pain Points:**
- Scoring algorithm questionable
- Inconsistent scores (search vs scan different)
- Over-penalizes "additives" not based on science
- Recommendations not available locally
- Bias toward European standards
- No transparency on methodology

**Opportunity:** Science-backed scoring using real papers, RAG-based recommendations

### 7. Splitwise - Expense Sharing
- **Revenue:** Est. $10-20M/year
- **Price:** Free (limited) / $4.99/month or $49.99/year
- **Problem Solved:** Split bills with friends
- **Trustpilot:** 1.9/5 ‚≠ê

**Top Pain Points:**
- 4 expenses/day limit on free (VERY HIGH)
- 10-second delay between entries (forced friction)
- Free version deliberately crippled
- Payment processing issues
- No phone support
- Reconciliation calculations don't match

**Opportunity:** GOLDMINE - TriCount/Settle Up stealing users. Simple splitter with reasonable limits wins.

---

### 8. Sleep Cycle - Sleep Tracker
- **Revenue:** Est. $30-50M/year
- **Price:** $39.99/year (no monthly!)
- **Problem Solved:** Wake during light sleep phase
- **Trustpilot:** 2.1/5 ‚≠ê

**Top Pain Points:**
- Charged for features already paid for (lifetime‚Üísubscription)
- Annual-only pricing
- Can't cancel subscription - errors
- Can't add naps or edit sleep data
- Apple Watch crashes

**Opportunity:** Monthly option, edit/add sleep data, clear comparisons

---

### 9. Flo - Period Tracker
- **Revenue:** ~$11M/MONTH üî• (2M downloads/month)
- **Price:** Free / $9.99/month or $49.99/year
- **Problem Solved:** Track menstrual cycle

**Top Pain Points:**
- Privacy nightmare - shared data with Facebook (class action!)
- $56M settlement for data selling
- App becoming cluttered
- Virtual assistant can't be turned off
- Used to be simple, now bloated

**Opportunity:** Privacy-first period tracker = MASSIVE opportunity. Trust destroyed in space.

---

### 10. Forest - Focus Timer
- **Revenue:** Est. $5-15M/year
- **Price:** $3.99 one-time (iOS) + premium
- **Problem Solved:** Stay off phone while working

**Top Pain Points:**
- Accidental app visit = dead tree at 99%
- No proper Pomodoro cycles
- Timer glitches
- No focus analytics
- Must manually start each session

**Opportunity:** Forgiving mode, auto Pomodoro, focus analytics
